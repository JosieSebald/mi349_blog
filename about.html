Hi, my name is Josie Sebald! I'm a cisgender woman and my pronouns are she/her. I'm a 19 year old college junior at Michigan State University. My major is in Games and Interactive Media, with a focus on animation. I'm an artist, gamer, lover of music, and a supporter of LGBT+.



As an artist, I am currently accepting commissions! If you'd like to order a commission, payment can be made through:

  • Paypal
  • CashApp
  • Venmo

I have experience in 3D design, 2D digital and traditional art (drawing and painting), though I am most comfortable with digital design. I use Krita on Windows and/or Procreate for iOS, depending on the commission type.
